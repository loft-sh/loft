Loft supports all major Single-Sign-On (SSO) providers. To configure SSO for Loft, expand the
desired SSO provider (e.g. Google, Okta, GitHub) field listed below and follow the instructions
provided.

:::caution Domain Required
Most SSO provider connections do not allow `localhost` and require that you
[set up a domain for your Loft instance](../getting-started/domain.mdx) first.
:::
